About me

Greetings! I'm Sanjid, presently an undergraduate at Shahjalal University of Science and Technology. My scientific interests lie in the field of Atmospheric Physics, where I seek to unravel the mysteries of our climate.

Within the realm of atmospheric physics, my interests span a wide spectrum, for example, extreme events, tropical cyclones, climate dynamics,heatwaves, the application of machine learning to climate physics, and more.What truly captivates my interest is the intersection of classical climate dynamics and cutting-edge machine learning techniques to seek novel solutions to pressing climate challenges.

I'm currently fervently engaged in three fascinating research projects: the first involves using machine learning to forecast the trajectory of tropical cyclones in the Bay of Bengal, a region known for its meteorological dynamism; the second involves using machine learning to forecast heatwaves in Bangladesh; and the third involves conducting a thorough analysis of extreme weather events in Bangladesh.
